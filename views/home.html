About Naba Rugs

Influential, innovative and progressive, Naba Rugs is reinventing a wholly modern approach to carpets, creating masterpieces using exceptional design skills and only the finest raw materials.

Naba Rugs was established in the year 1995 and in a span of 20 years has become the leading manufacturer and Exporter of carpets and rugs. Eclectic, contemporary, romantic NabaRugs products represent the pinnacle of Indian craftsmanship and are unsurpassed for their quality and attention to detail.

Loom-knotted, Hand-tuffted, Indo-Nepali, shaggy and Indo-Gabhe in different colours, design and quality (silk, wool and polyester) are some of the most popular types of carpets. Browse the whole collection here.
